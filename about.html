About Moodle

Moodle is a Learning Management System (LMS) designed for online education and distance learning.

It allows teachers to create online lessons, assignments and quizzes.

Students can join courses, upload homework and communicate online.

Teacher Opportunities

Teachers can manage courses and track student progress.
